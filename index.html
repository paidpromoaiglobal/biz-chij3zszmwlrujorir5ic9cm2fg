About Us

Sigma Lifestyle & Fitness Studio

Sigma Lifestyle and Fitness Studio is Kelambakkam’s premier gym, located on Rajiv Gandhi Salai opposite Croma. With separate floors for upper and lower body training, state-of-the-art equipment, and a clean, well-maintained environment, we provide everything you need to achieve your fitness goals.

Our team of expert trainers delivers personalised guidance to members at every fitness level — from beginners to advanced athletes. Rated 4.9 out of 5 with over 576 reviews, we are trusted by hundreds of members across Kelambakkam and the surrounding areas.
