About Us

SIGMA LIFESTYLE AND FITNESS STUDIO KELAMBAKKAM | GYM

Sigma Lifestyle and Fitness Studio is Kelambakkam's premier fitness destination, located conveniently on Rajiv Gandhi Salai, opposite Croma. With a stellar 4.9-star rating backed by over 576 reviews, we have earned the trust of hundreds of fitness enthusiasts across the community. Our facility features separate floors dedicated to upper and lower body workouts, ensuring a focused and efficient training experience for every member.

At Sigma, we believe fitness should be accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. Our gym is meticulously maintained, equipped with modern and well-serviced machines, and staffed by knowledgeable, friendly, and supportive trainers who are committed to helping you achieve your personal fitness goals. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned athlete, Sigma Lifestyle and Fitness Studio is the place where your transformation begins.
